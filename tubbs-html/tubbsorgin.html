Tubbs's Birth

Tubbs was born fat. He was born under the care of Mubbs, Tubb's mom, and Hubbs, Tubbs's uncle. He started to roll at two months, and started to walk at eleven months. His walking was delayed by his fatness.

Baby Tubbs

As a baby, Tubbs learned that when he ate, his fatness increased. He also discovered that cheezeboggers were nutritious to cats like him.
